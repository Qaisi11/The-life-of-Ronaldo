About Cristiano Ronaldo's career

Cristiano Ronaldo is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. He began his career at Sporting Lisbon in Portugal before moving to Manchester United in 2003. During his time at Manchester United, he won three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League. In 2009, he moved to Real Madrid where he established himself as one of the best players in the world, winning four more Champions League titles and four Ballon d'Or awards. In 2018, Ronaldo moved to Juventus in Italy, where he continues to play at the highest level. Throughout his career, Ronaldo has been known for his incredible speed, technical ability, and goalscoring prowess.
