About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Saudi Professional League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.

Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985 which means he is 38 years old.
